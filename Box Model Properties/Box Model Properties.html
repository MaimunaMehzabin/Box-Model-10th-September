Pizza

Pizza (Italian: ['pittsa'], Neapolitan: ['pittse]) is a savory dish of I talian origin consisting of a usually round, flattend base leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat etc.) , which is then baked at a high temeperature, taditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzeta. A person who makes pizza is known as pizzaiolo.
